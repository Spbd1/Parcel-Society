# Data Dictionary

CSV exports are not implemented in the foundation release. Future exports should be documented here before analysis begins.

Expected export domains include:

- Players and anonymous participant identifiers
- Servers and treatment assignments
- Parcels and parcel quality
- Round-level decisions
- Contracts and contract outcomes
- Institutional events
- Public treasury and aggregate outcomes
- Exit and final outcome measures

Every field should include a stable name, type, allowed values, missingness rule, and analysis note.
